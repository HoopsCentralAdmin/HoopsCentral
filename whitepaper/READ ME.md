# HoopsCentral Whitepaper

This directory contains the HoopsCentral whitepaper and long-form documentation explaining the vision and architecture of the project.

The whitepaper covers:

• Vision for a global basketball participation economy  
• Platform architecture built on Polygon  
• Creator and player incentive models  
• Challenge and competition mechanics  
• Community governance potential  

HoopsCentral aims to bring the global basketball community on-chain by rewarding skill, competition, and creativity through decentralized technology.
